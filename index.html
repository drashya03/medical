Medicine Connect

Connecting medical stores with near-expiry medicines to those with high sales potential

About Us

Our platform helps medical stores reduce waste by connecting them with other stores that can sell near-expiry medicines quickly. This ensures that medicines are used effectively and customers can access affordable healthcare.

How It Works

Step 1: List Near-Expiry Medicines

Stores can list their near-expiry medicines on our platform, providing details such as the medicine name, quantity, expiry date, and price.

Step 2: Find a Buyer

Our system matches the listed medicines with stores that have high sales potential and can sell them quickly.

Step 3: Complete the Transaction

Once a match is found, stores can complete the transaction securely, ensuring that medicines are distributed efficiently.
